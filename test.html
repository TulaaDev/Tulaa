WHO WE ARE

FOCUSED ON DELIVERING SCALABLE SOCIAL IMPACT IN RURAL AFRICA

Launched in 2016, Tulaa is connecting rural consumers and producers in Africa through a mobile commerce platform and using mobile money. We are scaling in Kenya and Ghana with both end-to-end and white labels solutions for our customers. Our vision in the long-term is to become the leading virtual market-place for rural Africa. We are a for-profit social enterprise supported by both private institutional and individual investors. As a growing social business, Tulaa is committed to reinvesting the majority of its returns into the business, to scale quickly and maximize its impact on rural communities.
